Impact

Impact of gastritis and prevention

H. Pilory is the most common cause of the chronic form of gastritis. In the EU/US it’s spread among 10% of 20-year olds and 50% of 50-year olds, increasing disposition to a host of serious chronic disease. H. Pilory is a Class I carcinogen, same as cigarettes. Digitally-assisted discovery and treatment steps allow young people to now take preventive action easily.

Impact of IBS and IBD

Irritable Bowel Syndrome - Treatment is highly individual taking into account many factors such as stress, physical activity, diet and sleep. Until very recently the only surefire way to distinguish between IBS and IBD were invasive and expensive Inflammatory Bowel Disease - Calprotectin biomarker now allows effective screening for IBD. Furthermore, this marker is well-proven in IBD management - as a mucosal health indicator, a predictor of disease relapse, and for projecting the therapeutic response.
